About Me

Hello, Economics students! I am Muhadeen Mohammed, a dedicated Economics student at the University of Ghana and a passionate Aspirant for UGESS President 2025. My journey in this department has shown me the academic struggles students face; finding the right study materials, accessing past questions, and collaborating effectively.

This is why I'm not just making promises, I'm taking action. Through this platform, I am providing free, well-organized Economics resources for all students, ensuring that learning is accessible, structured, and effective. My vision is simple: a student society that actively supports your academic and professional growth.

Vote for me, and let's build an Economics department where everyone thrives!

What You'll Find Here

This website serves as your one-stop resource center for Economics course materials:

  • Lecture slides for all Economics courses
  • Past examination questions with sample answers where available
  • Recommended textbooks and journal articles
  • Study guides and summary notes
  • Tutorial materials and practice problems

All resources are organized by year level, semester, and course code for seamless navigation, ensuring you can quickly find exactly what you need for your studies.
